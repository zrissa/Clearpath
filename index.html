Why I built this

I was the person
Clearpath is built for.

In 2017 I co-founded LK St. Clair Soccer — Macomb County's first MLS GO nonprofit soccer club. I was the coach, the admin, the grant writer, the registration desk, and the compliance officer. All at once.

I learned field permits by cold-calling parks departments. I learned grant writing by failing first. There was no platform that understood what I was trying to do. Clearpath is what I needed then.
